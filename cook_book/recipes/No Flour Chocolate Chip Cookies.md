# No Flour Chocolate Chip Cookies
Ingredients
---

 - 3 bananas
 - 1/3 cup apple sauce
 - 2 cups oats
 - 1/4 cup almond milk
 - 1/4 cup raisens or nuts
 - 1/4 cup dark chocolate chunks
 - 1 teaspoon vanilla
 - 1 teaspoon cinnamon

</br>

Instructions
---
 1. Pre-Heat Oven to 350 F / 176 C
 2. Mash bananas
 3. Add all other ingredients and mix until homogenous.
 4. Bake 15-20 minutes

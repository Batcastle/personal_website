3D Printing


So far, I haven't done too much with 3D printing. But, here's some basic info:

  • Printer: CR-10S Pro V2
  • Plastics tried: PLA, ABS, PETG
    • PLA is great for most basic projects
    • PETG is great for anything needing high-strength or heat resistance. If something's going to be abused somehow, use PETG.
    • DO NOT USE ABS. It's extreamly finicky, hates to stick to itself, and weaker than PETG (albeit minimally so).
